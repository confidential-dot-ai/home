To rotate the CDS image digest:

  helm upgrade {{ .Release.Name }} <chart> \
    --set cds.image.digest=sha256:<new> \
    --set cds.image.reference=ghcr.io/<owner>/cds:<new>

The post-install/post-upgrade hook re-fires and pushes the new digest into the
CDS-node plugin's unix socket. The plugin persists the payload to
{{ .Values.hostPaths.cacheDir }}/pushed.json so it survives restarts.

To uninstall and revert containerd patches:

  helm uninstall {{ .Release.Name }}
